Why smoke alarms
are essential?

Early detection
saves lives

Smoke alarms warn you of a fire
at the earliest possible moment,
providing crucial time to escape.

Significantly
Reduces Fire
Fatalities:

Homes without working smoke
alarms face a fire fatality rate
two to three times greater than
those with functional alarms.

24/7 Protection

Smoke alarms remain vigilant
around the clock, ready to alert
you as soon as they detect
smoke, even when you are
asleep.

Prevents Smoke
Inhalation Deaths

Most fire-related deaths result
from inhaling toxic gases,
not burns. Early warnings from
smoke alarms help prevent this.
